Curious what the port types are? Most miners are best served by PPLNS, which helps to combat pool hopping and ensures a good payout for miners. PPS is the most stable option of the lot, ensuring that you get paid out for every share, but with a higher fee for the risk the pool takes. Solo ports are for those who like to gamble and want to try and find their own block!
